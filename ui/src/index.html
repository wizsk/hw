Here are some tips:
  1. The Magnifin icon is for root search.
  2. The other guffy looking one is for full text search. Searching the whole dictionary for matching phrases.
  3. Press "SHIFT + Enter" or "CTRL + Enter" for full text search.
  4. Press "s" or "i" to hop into the search bar.
  5. Press "j" to scroll up.
  6. Press "k" to scroll down.
